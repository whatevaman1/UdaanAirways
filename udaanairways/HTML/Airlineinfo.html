About Udaan Airways

Our Journey

First Journey:

Udaan Airways took off with our first flight on July 28, 2006, connecting New Delhi to Imphal via Guwahati.

Launch of International Flights:

After five years of serving India, we earned permission to soar internationally, expanding horizons for our passengers.

Leading Airline in India:

In August 2012, Udaan Airways became India’s market leader, setting new standards in customer satisfaction and innovation.

Pioneering Daily Flights:

By December 2019, we proudly became the first Indian airline to operate 1,500 daily flights, backed by a fleet of over 250 aircraft.

Why Choose Udaan Airways?

Your Preferred Airline:

Udaan Airways blends unbeatable fares with premium experiences, making every journey extraordinary. From seamless online booking to live flight updates and a user-friendly app, we are transforming the way India flies.

Empowering Our Team:

Our employee-centric culture drives us. The ‘ifly’ training facility provides real-time learning, ensuring our team delivers top-notch service and enjoys a balanced work-life experience.

Commitment to Safety:

With Udaan Airways, safety isn’t just a priority; it's our foundation. Our rigorously trained pilots, engineers, and crew members make every flight a secure and pleasant journey.

Performance Excellence:

Every interaction with our team reflects our commitment to excellence, ensuring a comfortable experience from takeoff to landing. Fly with confidence, knowing you’re in capable hands.
